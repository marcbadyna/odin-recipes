Hard Boiled Eggs

Description

Quick and easy hard boiled eggs.

For a delicious, protein packed meal!

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Boiling water

Steps

  1. Bring water to a boil.
  2. When water is at a boil, carefully place whole eggs in the boiling water with a spoon.
  3. Let eggs boil in the water for 10 minutes
  4. After 10 minutes, remove from heat and let cold water from the sink fill the hot pot with eggs still in.
  5. When eggs and pot have cooled, crack eggs, dispose of shells, and enjoy!
